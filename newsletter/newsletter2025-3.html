Aday - June 21st


It might already feel very soon, but Aday is coming up! For this year's edition (which will be at the beginning of summer instead of at the end) we will be playing the Prison Island at All-in 040: an exciting game with all kinds of puzzles, games, and challenges! Afterwards, we are moving to the TU/e campus to enjoy a nice barbecue with more than enough time to network with your fellow alumni.

Where: All In 040 and the TU/e campus
When: Saturday June 21st 13:30 (Prison Island)
The barbecue starts around 17:00
Costs: €25 (Prison Island) and €16,50 (BBQ)
