Hello everyone! With the new year come many things: new life goals, a new fitness subscription, maybe even a new job. But one of the most important things that should come are new memories made with old (or new) friends! What better way than to join us at the New Year's alumni drink at GEWIS? The drink will take place on Friday the 24th (we've unfortunately had to postpone due to the cyber attack), starting at 16:30. So tell you friends, and see you there!
