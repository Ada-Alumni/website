Hi fellow alumni,


This is the eigth newsletter of Ada Alumni! Since the last newsletter, the Dies Pubquiz and our fourth and last VrijMiBo of the academic year 2023-2024 took place.

In this newsletter, you can find more information about the upcoming Aday Bouldering & BBQ activity, the new VrijMiBo dates of the upcoming academic year, as well as an Ada board change announcement.

Do you have something interesting to share via the newsletter? Do you have a good idea for the next activity or do you want to organize something yourself? Let us know!

Kind regards,

On behalf of Ada Alumni

Aday 2024 - Bouldering & BBQ


Ada-member day (Aday) is coming up again! During this day, you have ample opportunity to catch up with fellow alumni. This year we will go bouldering in the afternoon in Veldhoven, after which there is a BBQ at the TU/e campus!

We would love to see you and your study friends there, so register here.!

P.S. You can also register for only bouldering or only BBQ!

New VrijMiBo dates


The second and third Ada Alumni Vrijmibo were once again a great success. On January 19th, we celebrated the start of a new year, whilst enjoying a glass of champagne. Last VrijMiBo on April 5th included a variety of Easter-themed snacks to still your appetite next to drinking your favourite beverage. We hope you enjoyed it as much as us.

  • Q1 2024-2025: October 25th 2024
  • Q2 2024-2025: January 17th 2025
  • Q3 2024-2025: April 4th 2025
  • Q4 2024-2025: June 20th 2025

Our last VrijMiBo of this academic year will take place on the 21st of June. So be sure to save the date in your agenda if you don't want to miss out!

Changes in our board


In the past months, some changes have occured in our board.

Two new board members have joined our ranks, namely Anne Nijsten and Bram Wieringa. They, together with the rest of the board, will do their best to organize even more fun activities for you!

On a sadder note, two of the board members who have been there since the founding of Ada, Laura Kuntze and Floris Tulner, have unfortunately left our ranks. We would like to thank them for everything they have done for Ada in the past years!

If you are interested in joining the board, please send us an email!

Looking for new board members


Dear fellow alumni,

Ada will soon turn 2 years old on April 14. With that, some Ada board members are thinking of leaving the board in the near future. Therefore, Ada is looking for alumni who are interested in becoming a board member of Ada Alumni.

As the board, we come together once every month to have our board meeting of about an hour. Next to this, we usually also spend one hour on average on doing our action points.

So, if you are interested in joining the board or want to know more about what we do as a board, please send us an email! We will gladly explain to you about what it actually means to be a board member of Ada Alumni.

With kind regards,

The board of Ada Alumni
