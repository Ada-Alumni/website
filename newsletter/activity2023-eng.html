Activity April 2023 - Petanque

Dear alumni,

It's almost that time of the year; we happily announce our first birthday! On the 14th of April, it's already 1 year ago that Ada Alumni was officially founded. In the last newsletter, we announced an upcoming social event and this message contains more information about this event.

When: 14 of April, Opening: 19.00.
Where: Dommelstraat 7 - 5611 CJ Eindhoven
Special: From 20.00 till 22.00, you are able to show off your Jeu de Boules (petanque) skills, which you may know from camping trips or the yearly activity of one of the fraternities of GEWIS.

To get an indication of the number of party people we ask you to register here. Your partner is welcome as well! Please, send us an email to let us know if your partner is joining.

We are looking forward to seeing you!

Ada Alumni
