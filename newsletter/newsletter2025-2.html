Dies Activity: Pubquiz - April 12th


It's already been 3 years since we were founded. To celebrate this fact together with you all, we are organizing a pub quiz! The quiz will take place on April 12th at GEWIS. You can either join with a pre-formed team, or create one on the spot, and meet new friends!

Please let us know if you're coming by joining the whatsapp group here. Hope to see you all there!

Alumni Startup Night - March 31st


On Monday March 31st, the annual Alumni Startup Night in Eindhoven will take place at the Eindhoven University of Technology campus. The event celebrates the region's potential for innovation and collaboration, where many successful startups have emerged. The evening also highlights the importance of knowledge sharing between the academic entrepreneurial networks of Eindhoven University of Technology, Tilburg University, The Gate, IQONIC, Round One, TIAS, and JADS.

This year's theme, Innovation in Healthcare, will feature inspiring stories from alumni-entrepreneurs. There will also be a networking part of the evening, using the Conversation Starter platform, which will allow you to make the most meaningful connections.

If you are interested you can sign up here!
