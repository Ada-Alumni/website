Hi fellow alumnus,


The first newsletter of Ada Alumni is a fact! Thank you for signing up to our network. In this way we will keep you informed of news and activities in which you can participate.

Do you have something interesting to share via the newsletter? Do you have a good idea for the next activity or do you want to organize something yourself? Let us know!

Kind regards,

On behalf Ada Alumni

First activity


Our first activity will take place on September 17th starting at 16:30h and ending no later than 20:00h at study association GEWIS (MF3.155). During this activity you can catch up with former fellow students while enjoying a drink! We will provide all members with some refreshments and the opportunity to retrieve old memories. So save a spot in your calendar for our first reunion drink and register here!

Your help is needed to make the activity a great success! Invite your former fellow classmates to join Ada Alumni, and have a nice chat over a drink in September!

Website


Did you know that Ada Alumni has her own website? You can visit it via ada-alumni.nl! On the website you will find more information about how this initiative started, and what our goal is.

Discord


Since you have signed up for Ada Alumni, you can also use our Discord. You can join a group created especially for your generation. Is your generation not yet listed? Send us an email and we will add it.
