Hi fellow alumni,


This is already the seventh newsletter of Ada Alumni! In the past few months since the last newsletter, the WinTU/ervillage Christmas Drink and our second and third VrijMiBo took place.

In this newsletter, you can find more information about the upcoming Pubquiz Dies activity, the remaining VrijMiBo of this academic year, a save the date for the second Aday, info about discount cards for Stadscafé Spijker, as well as an open invitation to becoming an Ada board member.

Do you have something interesting to share via the newsletter? Do you have a good idea for the next activity or do you want to organize something yourself? Let us know!

Kind regards,

On behalf of Ada Alumni

Ada Dies Activity - Pubquiz


Ada's next birthday is coming up soon! To celebrate this occasion, we organise a pubquiz at GEWIS on Sunday April 21 at 15:00h. Do you want to show off your knowledge on random topics? Or do you just want to catch up with study friends? Then this is the perfect activity for you! Please let us know you are coming by joining the WhatsApp group in the community.

You can join the WhatsApp group here.

We hope to see you there!

VrijMiBo


The second and third Ada Alumni Vrijmibo were once again a great success. On January 19th, we celebrated the start of a new year, whilst enjoying a glass of champagne. Last VrijMiBo on April 5th included a variety of Easter-themed snacks to still your appetite next to drinking your favourite beverage. We hope you enjoyed it as much as us.

Our last VrijMiBo of this academic year will take place on the 21st of June. So be sure to save the date in your agenda if you don't want to miss out!

Aday - Save the Date!


On the 7th of September, our second Aday will take place! Last year, we went to Oirschot to play Pitch & Putt. After everyone had finished playing 18 holes, we enjoyed a lovely barbecue to close off the sunny day.

We are still brainstorming on the activity for this year. But for now, please make sure to block September 7th in your agenda. Hopefully see you then!

Spijker discount cards for Ada members


Recently, the GEWIS board has informed us that it is possible for Ada members to get discount cards for Stadscafé Spijker at Stratum. By showing this card at the bar at Stadscafé Spijker, you will receive a discount on the drinks that you have ordered.

If you would like to have a Spijker discount card, or receive more information on these cards, please reach out to the Ada board by sending us an email.

Looking for new board members


Dear fellow alumni,

Ada will soon turn 2 years old on April 14. With that, some Ada board members are thinking of leaving the board in the near future. Therefore, Ada is looking for alumni who are interested in becoming a board member of Ada Alumni.

As the board, we come together once every month to have our board meeting of about an hour. Next to this, we usually also spend one hour on average on doing our action points.

So, if you are interested in joining the board or want to know more about what we do as a board, please send us an email! We will gladly explain to you about what it actually means to be a board member of Ada Alumni.

With kind regards,

The board of Ada Alumni
