Ada Dies Activity - Pubquiz


Ada's next birthday is coming up soon! To celebrate this occasion, we organise a pubquiz at GEWIS on Sunday April 21 at 15:00h. Do you want to show off your knowledge on random topics? Or do you just want to catch up with study friends? Then this is the perfect activity for you! Please let us know you are coming by joining the WhatsApp group in the community.

You can join the WhatsApp group here.

We hope to see you there!

VrijMiBo


The second and third Ada Alumni Vrijmibo were once again a great success. On January 19th, we celebrated the start of a new year, whilst enjoying a glass of champagne. Last Friday's VrijMiBo included a variety of Easter-themed snacks to still your appetite next to drinking your favourite beverage. We hope you enjoyed it as much as us.

Our last VrijMiBo of this academic year will take place at the 21st of June. So be sure to save the date in your agenda if you don't want to miss out!

We hope to see you on June 21st!
