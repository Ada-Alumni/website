Activity September 2023 - Pitch & Putt and BBQ

Dear alumni,

The first annual Ada-Members Day, a.k.a. Aday will take place on the second Saturday of September. Make sure you don't miss out on this nice event and make sure to register!

When: September 9, Opening: 14.30.
Where: Spoordonkseweg 80 - 5688 KE Oirschot
Special: From 15.00h till 17.30h, you are able to show off your Pitch & Putt skills. At 17.30h, the BBQ will start.

To participate, everyone needs to register before September 1 via this link. Your partner is welcome as well!

Don't forget to invite your fellow alumni as well!

We are looking forward to seeing you!
