Hi fellow alumni,


This is already the fourth newsletter of Ada Alumni! Over the past few months, we have celebrated our first Dies. During the activity organized on this special day, alumni had the chance to catch up with each other while playing a game of petanque.

That having said, we are looking forward to future activities, of which the first one is right around the corner! In this newsletter, you can find more about our next activity!

Do you have something interesting to share via the newsletter? Do you have a good idea for the next activity or do you want to organize something yourself? Let us know!

Kind regards,

On behalf of Ada Alumni

Aday - Pitch & Putt and BBQ


Saturday September 9th, the first Annual Ada-Members Day, a.k.a. Aday, is organized. This day will be a fun day with activities with your fellow alumni!

The day will take place at the Komaen complex (address: Spoordonkseweg 80, Oirschot). We will gather from 14:30 on, and our first activity, Pitch and Putt, will start at 15:00.

After the Pitch & Putt, a barbeque will be held at the same location, starting at 17:30. At 20:30, the official program will be over, and you are free to close the day wherever you want!

Already looking forward to joining us on September 9th? Fill out this form! Please, let all your fellow alumni know about the day, and hopefully we will see you on the 9th of September!

Alumni Night BCS


With 50 participating alumni and more than 250 computer science bachelor students who interviewed alumni on their careers, it was again a successful event in 2022!

This year, we will have a physical Alumni Night again. It is scheduled for the evening of Tuesday November 28, 2023. Before the event starts, there will be a buffet for alumni.

We hope you would like to join us (again) this year. If you want to join, please fill out this Google Forms form.

Please note: if you already participated last year, we ask you to not fill in this form. We will invite you later personally, with a pre-filled in forms, which saves you (and us) work :)

Kind regards

Wietske de Bondt
Projectteam Alumni Night 2023

Prolonging Membership GEWIS


Dear Alumni,

Recently GEWIS sent out an email to renew your membership. By renewing your membership, you will stay able to log in to your GEWIS account and join activities from GEWIS. So be sure to renew your account before the first of July if you want to stay a GEWIS member! This does not cost you anything except time to click a link and check your account details.

If you haven't received this email, then this might be because it was sent to your student email which doesn't exist anymore. If this is the case, be sure to email me (the secretary) if you want to renew your membership!

Sincerely,

Daan Verkade
Secretary of GEWIS 2022/2023

UFE Impact Night: June 28, 2023


The first UFe Impact Night will take place on Wednesday, June 28, 2023! A special gathering where a number of projects are presented that have been made possible by alumni. Everyone who wants to know more about the Eindhoven University Fund is very welcome! We will show you that your contribution really matters and take you along with us in beautiful, new developments, including a presentation by Prof.dr. Maarten Merkx on a new rapid ‘glow-in-the-dark’ test for infectious diseases. The event will take place in Dutch. More information and the program can be found on their website.
