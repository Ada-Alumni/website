VrijMiBo


Dear alumnus,

Remember the Friday Afternoon Borrel or VrijMiBo? Do you want to go to the VrijMiBo again at GEWIS, but feel like you don't know people anymore?

Ada Alumni, in collaboration with GEWIS and the BAC, wants to facilitate the VrijMiBo at the end of each quartile, so alumni can go and know there are other alumni to catch up with and have a drink with. Each quartile this will be the last Friday of the quartile before the exam weeks, to wish students good luck with their exams and celebrate we don't have any exams anymore.

The bar committee of GEWIS has even ordered a special craft beer which does not appear often at GEWIS! Want to find out which one? Come to the VrijMiBo on Friday October 27 and find out! We hope to see you there!

P.S. The VrijMiBo will as always be self-service. This means you must order and pay for drinks yourself using the SudoSOS screens behind the bar. Make sure to activate your SudoSOS account beforehand and update the balance. Want a glass to go with your drink? Ask a BAC member and clean, dry and store it again after use.

GEWIS Skiing Trip


Dear alumni,

We have a great offer for those of you who love the slopes! GEWIS organises a skiing trip to Austria this February and they have some spots left for alumni. You can join this week-long ski trip for only €620-650 including transfer, stay, 6-day skipass, and some fun side activities like toboggan (rodelen) and a dinner! All ski or snowboard materials can be rented for only €70 euros.

You can find more information and the sign-up list here. If you want to join, please register before November 1st!

Innovation Night


Help the future of innovation

In TU/e's Student Teams Program, annually ∼600 students of TU/e challenge themselves to tackle big societal problems with cutting-edge technology-spanning themes like mobility, urban environment, energy, biosensing, AI, and robotics. Developing (often) industry-leading proof of concepts, the teams aim to inspire scalable change, establish spin-off ventures, as well as achieve personal growth as The Engineers of the Future.

Every year, our alumni and GURUs help our students tackle complex problems, develop themselves, and ultimately help them achieve amazing outcomes. You complement the passion and energy of our student teams and start-ups with experience and networks.

Join us and help our student teams

Students will showcase their newest projects at TU/e Innovation Night on November 7th from 17:00 to 20:00. We invite you to join them as our special guest and to find out how you can contribute to their important work by becoming a GURU. As a GURU your experience and your network help to accelerate their important work.
