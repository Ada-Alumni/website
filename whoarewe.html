Who are we?

We are a foundation welcoming alumni of the Mathematics and Computer Science (M&CS) department at Eindhoven University of Technology, founded in April 2022. Our goal is to facilitate our members with a network of alumni in the same field. This way, we want to create possibilities to stay in touch with each other, even after graduating.

On this website, you can find more information about our initiative. We aim to organize multiple activities throughout the year. The first activity that is on our agenda is a reunion. In addition to that, we hope to organize some other events like networking activities and lectures about recent developments in mathematics and computer science. These activities will be announced in our newsletter.

If you still have questions, you can contact us at alumni@gewis.nl.
