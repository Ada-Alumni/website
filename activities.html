  • Aday - Pitch & Putt and BBQ

    September 9 at 14:30.
    Spoordonkseweg 80, Oirschot

    Saturday September 9th, the first Annual Ada-Members Day, a.k.a. Aday, is organized. This day will be a fun day with activities with your fellow alumni! The day will take place at the Komaen complex. We will gather from 14:30 on, and our first activity, Pitch and Putt, will start at 15:00.

    After the Pitch & Putt, a barbeque will be held at the same location, starting at 17:30. At 20:30, the official program will be over, and you are free to close the day wherever you want!

  • Dies Ada - Petanque at Boules & Bites

    April 14 starting at 19:00h.
    Boules & Bites, Dommelstraat 7, 5611CJ Eindhoven

    To celebrate the Dies of Ada Alumni, we have arranged some bowling alleys (between 20:00h and 22:00h), and you can catch up with fellow alumni while enjoying a game of petanque.

  • X-mas Market at TU/e

    December 13 starting at 17:30h.
    TU/e - Atlas Building, Groene Loper 5, 5612AE Eindhoven

    The second activity of Ada Alumni will be taking place at the X-mas market of the TU/e. Here, there will be a special guest updating you on recent developments in our research areas and within the department. Furthermore, you will be able to catch up with fellow alumni, and receive a collector's item gift.

  • Kick-off activity: Reunion Social Drink

    September 17 starting at 16:30h.
    MF3.155, Groene Loper 5, 5612AE Eindhoven

    The first activity of Ada Alumni was a social drink at GEWIS where you had the opportunity to reunite with your study friends.
